Compliance Landscape
Who's following the $50k rule?
O. Reg. 476/24 limits salary range width to $50,000 for jobs under $200k. High-compensation roles (annual max >$200k) are fully exempt from disclosure.

Methodology note: "Exempt" and "compliant" describe the disclosed salary data relative to the regulation. They do not evaluate actual employer pay practices, which may differ from posted ranges. Regulation source: Employment Standards Act, O. Reg. 476/24.

Professional context

A range width of ±15–25% of midpoint (30–50% total) is considered a standard salary band in most compensation frameworks. Ranges below 30% more likely represent a single pay point rather than a true band.
